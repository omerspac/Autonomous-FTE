# Business Goals 2026 - Autonomous FTE

## Core Product
**Autonomous FTE (Full-Time Employee)**: An AI-powered workforce automation system that handles perception, reasoning, and action layers autonomously.

## Target Audience
- Small to Medium Business Owners
- Operations Managers
- Solo-entrepreneurs looking to scale

## Value Proposition
- **24/7 Operation**: Never sleeps, always monitoring.
- **High Reliability**: Human-in-the-loop ensures quality.
- **Modular Scaling**: Add "Skills" like LinkedIn posting, email drafting, and calendar management.

## Q1 Goals
- Launch Silver Tier Functional Assistant.
- Acquire 50 beta testers.
- Establish authority in the "AI Agents" space on LinkedIn.
